Help Organize our 30 Year Reunion

Hi everyone!

If you'd like to help with organizing our 30 year reunion in 2022, please email Christian Schraga. There are lots of things you can do. Here are just a few of the tasks we'll need help with:

  • Finding and choosing a venue
  • Event planning
  • Managing RSVPs
  • Communications

More than anything else, right now we need to get in touch with as many of our classmates as possible to give them ample notice that we're having a reunion in late 2022. If all you do is get a few of our classmates to sign-up for our reunion mailing list, it'll already be a big help. You can also send a list of SHS '92 email addresses that you have to Sheldon Chang.
