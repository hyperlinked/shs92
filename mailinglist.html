2022 Reunion Updates

Hello Class of 92 Falcons!

Our 30 year reunion is quickly approaching. Even though it's not until 2022, we want to start the process now so that you can save the date.

Additionally, we want to gather everyone's permanent contact information so that we can find you even if you’re not on social networks. Therefore, we’ve decided to announce the reunion date over email to everyone who signs up for our mailing list.

So, please do the following three steps now:

  • Fill out the form on this page to sign up for the email list.
  • Look for an email from the reunion committee after the New Year.
  • Help spread the word.

We want to personally invite ALL of our classmates so please tell everyone from our class year that you still keep in touch with. We're including people who were in our class but didn't graduate with us. Please refer them to shs92reunion.com or ask them to email Sheldon Chang (sheldon@socialwave.com) with their info.

Also, if you want to help, you can join the reunion committee.
